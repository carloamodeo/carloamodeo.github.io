About Me

I am an algorithms engineer at neuro42, a medtech ULF MRI startup based in SF, California. I am passionate about technology, and in particular about how this can be used to improve people's lives.

As an engineer with expertise in signal processing, my impact can be huge in the field of diagnosis, whether it is through medical imaging technologies or through wearable devices. Thanks to my double Master's obtained between Italy and US I have a deep understanding of the weaknesses and the strengths of the medical device strategies in the two continents.

My expertise is in designing algorithms, whether deterministic or ML-based, to maximize the performance of medical devices. Take a look at my projects to get an idea!
